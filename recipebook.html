  • South Indian Dosa

    Ingredients:

    Rice, Black gram, Salt, Water, Oil

    Instructions:

    1. Soak rice and black gram separately for 6 hours. 2. Grind them into a smooth batter. 3. Ferment the batter overnight. 4. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladleful of batter, and spread it into a thin circle. 5. Drizzle oil around the edges. 6. Cook until golden brown. 7. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

  • North Indian Butter Chicken

    Ingredients:

    Chicken, Butter, Yogurt, Cream, Tomato puree, Ginger, Garlic, Spices

    Instructions:

    1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger, garlic, and spices for 2 hours. 2. Grill or roast the chicken until cooked. 3. In a pan, melt butter, add tomato puree, cream, and cooked chicken. 4. Simmer until the sauce thickens. 5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

  • Chinese Fried Rice

    Ingredients:

    Rice, Vegetables (carrot, peas, bell peppers), Eggs, Soy Sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Oil

    Instructions:

    1. Cook rice and let it cool. 2. Heat oil in a pan, add minced garlic and ginger. 3. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until tender. 4. Push vegetables to one side, scramble eggs on the other side. 5. Mix eggs with vegetables. 6. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, mix well. 7. Cook for a few minutes, stirring continuously. 8. Serve hot.

  • Chinese Chow Mein

    Ingredients:

    Noodles, Chicken/Beef/Shrimp, Vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers), Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Garlic, Ginger, Oil

    Instructions:

    1. Cook noodles according to package instructions. 2. Heat oil in a pan, add minced garlic and ginger. 3. Add protein and stir-fry until cooked. 4. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until tender. 5. Add cooked noodles, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. 6. Toss everything together until well combined. 7. Serve hot.

  • South Indian Idli

    Ingredients:

    Rice, Black gram (urad dal), Salt, Water

    Instructions:

    1. Soak rice and black gram separately for 4-6 hours. 2. Grind them into a smooth batter. 3. Ferment the batter overnight. 4. Grease idli molds and pour the batter into them. 5. Steam for about 10-12 minutes. 6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

  • North Indian Paneer Tikka

    Ingredients:

    Paneer (cottage cheese), Bell peppers, Onions, Yogurt, Spices (garam masala, turmeric, chili powder), Lemon juice

    Instructions:

    1. Cut paneer, bell peppers, and onions into cubes. 2. Mix yogurt with spices and lemon juice to make a marinade. 3. Marinate paneer and vegetables for 1-2 hours. 4. Skewer them alternately. 5. Grill or bake until golden brown. 6. Serve hot with mint chutney.

  • Chinese Hot and Sour Soup

    Ingredients:

    Chicken/vegetable broth, Tofu, Mushrooms, Bamboo shoots, Eggs, Vinegar, Soy sauce, Cornstarch, Pepper, Chili sauce

    Instructions:

    1. Bring broth to a boil, add tofu, mushrooms, and bamboo shoots. 2. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs. 3. Slowly pour whisked eggs into the boiling soup, stirring constantly. 4. Mix vinegar, soy sauce, cornstarch, pepper, and chili sauce in a bowl. 5. Slowly add this mixture to the soup, stirring continuously. 6. Simmer until the soup thickens. 7. Serve hot.

  • Hamburger

    Ingredients:

    Ground beef, Hamburger buns, Lettuce, Tomato, Onion, Pickles, Cheese, Ketchup, Mustard

    Instructions:

    1. Divide ground beef into patties, season with salt and pepper. 2. Grill or fry the patties until cooked to your preference. 3. Toast the hamburger buns. 4. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, cheese, ketchup, and mustard. 5. Serve hot.
